Everyone gets busy. But how do you make yourself to finish your work?

Routify allows users to gamify their experience of setting and completing goals. Anyone can set something to do that need to be completed later in the day or in the week with To-dos. Routify also allows users to set long term goals so that users can see their progress over the weeks as they finish reading that last book. Routify even lets users track habits, like drinking water, throughout the day. Users can join and compete against their friends as everyone completes more of their goals!
